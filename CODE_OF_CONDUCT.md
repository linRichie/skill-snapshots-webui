# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

## Our Standards

### Positive Behavior

* **Empathy**: We seek to understand and empathize with diverse perspectives and experiences.
* **Respect**: We value diverse opinions and acknowledge that different viewpoints contribute to our shared success.
* **Collaboration**: We work together to solve problems and create the best solutions.
* **Inclusion**: We create an environment where everyone feels welcome and valued.
* **Learning**: We are open to learning new ideas and adapting to change.

### Unacceptable Behavior

* **Harassment**: Any form of harassment, including but not limited to derogatory comments, slurs, or threats.
* **Discrimination**: Discrimination based on any protected characteristic.
* **Disruptive Behavior**: Actions that disrupt the community or its operations.
* **Unwelcome Conduct**: Any conduct that is inappropriate for a professional community.

## Our Responsibilities

### Project Maintainers

* Clarify and enforce our standards of acceptable behavior
* Take appropriate action when standards are violated
* Provide a fair and transparent process for resolving conflicts

### Contributors

* Follow these standards in all interactions
* Report incidents to the maintainers
- Help create a welcoming and inclusive community

## Enforcement

### Reporting Incidents

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact us at:
- Email: support@example.com
- GitHub Issues: https://github.com/your-username/skill-snapshots-webui/issues

### Consequences

When a report is received, we will:
1. Investigate the report thoroughly
2. Determine appropriate action
3. Respond to the reporter within a reasonable timeframe

Possible consequences include:
- Warning
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent ban from the community
